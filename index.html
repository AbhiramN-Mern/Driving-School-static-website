Our Story

Established in 2010, Mother Driving School has been serving the Kanhangad community for over 13 years. We started with a simple mission: to provide quality driving education that emphasizes safety, confidence, and skill development.

Our school has grown from a single instructor to a team of certified professionals, helping thousands of students achieve their driving goals. We believe that learning to drive should be a positive, stress-free experience that prepares you for a lifetime of safe driving.

Our Values

  • Safety First - Every lesson prioritizes road safety
  • Patient Instruction - We adapt to each student's learning pace
  • Professional Excellence - Certified instructors with ongoing training
  • Community Focus - Proud to serve Kanhangad and surrounding areas
